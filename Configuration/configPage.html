External Ratings

Shows Letterboxd ratings for movies and IMDB ratings for TV shows directly in Jellyfin.

🎬 Letterboxd (Movies)

Fetched by scraping letterboxd.com — no API key needed. Ratings are on a 5-star scale.

📺 IMDB (TV Shows)

Fetched via the free OMDB API. Get a free key here (1,000 req/day).

🎨 Display

⚡ Cache

Ratings are cached to avoid excessive requests. Clear to force a refresh.
